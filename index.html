About

I am a Research Scientist at Lyft working on matching algorithms to address efficiency and reliability in multi-sided markets. I completed my PhD in Operations Research at Columbia University and was fortunate enough to be advised by Professor Clifford Stein.

My research focuses on studying efficient algorithms in NP-hard matching settings. I am also interested in the theory of learning hidden graphs/hypergraphs structures, as well as graph partitoning problems.
